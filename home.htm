We are now moved in!

On Nov. 4, 2006, we moved into the Octabode and started enjoying the fruits of three years, three months, and three days of hard work, frustration, spending our life's saving, learning, and laughing. While there are still many things that need finishing, we can now rest briefly and take pride in our accomplishment.

Welcome to Octabode.com, the chronicle of our quest to build an interesting home, mostly by ourselves. The main purpose of this site is to let our family and friends keep tabs on our progress, view pictures, read a journal of commentary, and so forth. We hope that other folks might stumble across this site and find it interesting or inspiring.
